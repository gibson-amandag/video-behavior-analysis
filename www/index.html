Scoring Information

Playback and scoring controls

Keys:
  • Events: g = start/stop GROOMING, r = start/stop REARING
  • State: j = add state transition (switch state)
Use left/right arrows to step by this many seconds; hold Shift for ±1s jump

Keys to control playback:

  • Playback speed:
    • q=0.5x, w=1x, a=2x, s=3x
    • ↑ / ↓ to increase / decrease speed by the speed step
  • Step through: ← / → to step by Step size; hold Shift for ±1s jump
  • Seek / play: z = back 10s, x = play/pause, c = forward 10s
